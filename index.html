Biography

My research focuses on the intersection of neural engineering, artificial intelligence and computational clinical research, with a particular interest in deep learning, multimodal learning, brain-machine interfaces and physiological signal analysis. The goal of my research is to investigate how machine learning frameworks can be used to aid, understand and integrate multimodal clinical medical data.

Research Interests

  • Pattern recognition and deep learning in EEG signals
  • Multimodal data fusion for physiological signals
  • Closed-loop adaptive neuromodulation
